Upcoming Events

E3 2017

June 13-15th, 2017
Location: Los Angeles, California

With E3 2017 fast approaching it is a great opportunity to make new connections. Especially considering this will be the first year tickets have gone out to the public. Meet people and see the upcoming games and systems! What could be better?

PAX West 2017

September 1-4th, 2017
Location: Seattle, Washington

If you are from the Pacific Northwest then you may already know that VERY soon badges for this event will go on sale. PAX (Penny Arcade Expo) is one of the biggest gaming events of the year for the community. People fly from all over the country and even from outside it for this event so you best be prepared to mash that refresh button in your browser. These tickets go FAST but we hope to see you there.

Twitch Con 2017

October 20-22nd, 2017
Location: Long Beach, California

This is one of the final major events of the calendar year and the one that most of the pillars of the streaming community attend. If you watch games being played live, you know Twitch, and if you know Twitch you will definitley recognize some faces here.
